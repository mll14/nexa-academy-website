How to pay

Option 1 — Pay online (recommended). Sign in to your student dashboard{% if frontend_url %} at {{ frontend_url }}{% endif %} and choose Make a Payment. Pay by M-Pesa or card through Paystack: your balance updates instantly, this payment is reconciled automatically and your receipt is emailed to you the moment it clears — no confirmation message needed.

Option 2 — M-Pesa Lipa na M-Pesa (Paybill). On your phone open M-Pesa → Lipa na M-Pesa → Pay Bill, then enter:

Business No. (Paybill): {{ pay_paybill }} ({{ pay_bank }})
Account No.: {{ pay_account_number }}
Amount: the sum you wish to pay
Funds are received by {{ pay_account_name }}.

If you pay by Paybill, bank transfer or cash, forward the M-Pesa or bank confirmation message to {{ admissions_email }}{% if reference %}, quoting reference {{ reference }}{% endif %}, or submit it from the Record a payment tab on your dashboard so we can post it to your account.
